Wedding Ceremony

This wedding ceremony photography session was done at the wedding reception location at salt lake, in the heart of Kolkata. I had the pleasure of photographing the ceremony and reception of the beautiful bride and groom. It was my first time photograpicing. The ceremony was held in an elegant and beautiful chapel. This is a very important ceremony in a wedding. A ceremony is the time when the couple is married, where they are united, and where the marriage ceremony takes place. In my experience, this is one of my favorite ceremonies. As the groom, I am very proud of this beautiful ceremony.

Pre Wedding

Pre-wedding shoots are for those who want good pictures of togetherness; without the hassles of the heavy wedding attires, makeup, ornaments, umpteen number of people around, and the annoying selfies. Pre-wedding photography allows both partners to get to know each other better. You are free to pose or not to pose. Pre-wedding shoot photographers are intent on capturing natural moments, expressions, and real emotions shared by the couple. Such pictures make your album more vibrant.
